The Monster Burger

Monstrously tasty burgers are great for kid parties. Get kids into the kitchen to help build the silly sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 8 ground beef patties (4 oz each)
  • 8 burger buns, split
  • 8 slices (3/4 oz each) American cheese
  • 8 thin slices cooked ham
  • 16 slices dill pickle
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Step

    2. Set oven control to broil. Place ground beef patties on broiler pan. Broil with tops 3 to 4 inches from heat 10 to 12 minutes or until thoroughly cooked, turning once.

  2. Step

    3. Place bottom halves of buns on serving platter. For each monster burger, place 1 cooked patty on bottom half of bun. Cut cheese slice in half in zigzag pattern to look like teeth. Place half of cheese slice on burger, with "teeth" hanging off 1 side of patty.

  3. Step

    4. Loosely fold ham into tongue shape; place on top of "teeth." Place remaining cheese "teeth" on top of "tongue." Top with top half of bun. Place 2 pickle slices on top for eyes. Dot "eyes" with ketchup for pupils.

Nutrition

  • 420 Calories
  • 22g Total Fat
  • 32g Protein
  • 23g Total Carbohydrate
  • 4g Sugars
